Borrowing and Returning Books

To borrow a book, visit our library and find the desired book. Present your library card to the librarian for checkout.

To return a book, simply bring it back to the library and drop it in the designated return slot.

System Overview

Our book management system allows users to browse and borrow books from our library. Users can search for books by title, author, or category.

Books can be borrowed for a set period, and overdue books may incur fines. Users can also reserve books that are currently checked out by other users.
